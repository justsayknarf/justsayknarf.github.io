This site.

This site is being served with Jekyll, hosted on Github Pages.

This site is meant to be a fun platform for me to explore and learn. Continuing to add features bit by bit, pixel by pixel, page by page. I wanted to avoid Wordpress, Joomla, Squarespace, or any other CRM/website generator to be able to easily look behind the curtain without too much trouble, and be able to easily tweak without too much hand-holding.

The result is... pretty much what you're looking at. Various libraries, random tweaks here and there. All fun.

Some other random stuff:

Hang Drummer.

The result of playing around with the paper.js and howler.js libraries. Use keyboard (select letters and space bar) to interact.

This was a quick project after listening to a friend play the hang drum for the first time. It made me curious what it was about the hang drum that produced such calming sounds. A digital representation does not hold a modicum of light to the powerful serenity of the actual instrument, but I wanted to see what would happen if the similar sounds were used. I'm fairly positive that the notes in the sound samples that I found are not the standard tuning of most hang drums, but it was what I could find at the time.
